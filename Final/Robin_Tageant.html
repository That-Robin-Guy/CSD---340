Robin's Biography:

I was born in a small town in southeastern Louisiana named Franklinton, only known locally for it's "Free" State fair it holds every fall. I was born the cooler half of a set of twins. At seven years old, my dad surrendered to the ministry and became a pastor. He whisked the family away to Mississippi. Over the years until our colleges years we moved from church to church. My brother and I stated college attending the University of Mobile. I left college early to work full-time in retail, while he finished up his degree in Psychology. After he finished college, we moved in together. We lived together until he passed from a car accident in 2018. His death, while extremely difficult even now, has sparked a fire in me to keep moving. Keep progressing in life. This is part of the reason I have decided to go back to school to finish my bachelor's degree.
